About

The Laboratory for Intelligent Sensing and Control & Unmanned System (ISC&US LAB) at Nankai University was founded in July 2019 and currently brings together 29 researchers — 2 faculty members, 8 PhD students, and 19 master’s students.

Our research spans distributed reinforcement learning for multi-agent systems, data-driven control, game theory and optimization, and safe cooperative control, as well as autonomous unmanned systems (UAVs, UGVs): visual perception, autonomous localization, trajectory planning, and swarm cooperative control. To date the lab has published 50+ SCI papers and holds 2 invention patents and 4 software copyrights.

Within the lab, the Intelligent Sensing and Autonomous Navigation team (ISAN LAB) focuses on the sensing and navigation side of unmanned systems.
