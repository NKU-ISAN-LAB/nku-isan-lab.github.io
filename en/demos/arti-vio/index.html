ARTI-VIO: Asynchronous Multi-Camera Visual-Inertial Odometry

The video loads from the external player only after you press play.

Abstract

ARTI-VIO is an asynchronous multi-camera visual-inertial odometry system that supports heterogeneous, non-hardware-synchronized multi-camera rigs. It introduces a feature recall mechanism in the front-end and a time-interpolated optimization formulation in the back-end to fuse asynchronous observations from multiple camera groups without enforcing a global frame trigger. ARTI-VIO is built on top of VINS-Fusion and is released as open source under GPLv3.
