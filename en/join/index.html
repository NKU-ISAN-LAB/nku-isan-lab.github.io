Join Us

Prospective students start here

The group continuously recruits postdocs and, each year, admits about one PhD student and three master’s students. Direct-PhD, qualifying-exam PhD, recommended, and entrance-exam applicants — and anyone genuinely interested in our research directions — are welcome to get in touch.

Directions: postdocs focus on perception, localization, planning, and control for autonomous unmanned systems; PhD and master’s applicants may apply through Control Science & Engineering, Intelligent Science & Technology, Operations Research & Cybernetics, or Artificial Intelligence.

Background: automation, intelligence, computer science, electronics, mechanical engineering, applied mathematics, or a related field.

We especially welcome students with a genuine interest in research, a solid mathematical foundation, and a diligent, persistent, self-motivated attitude.

Contact: send your CV and transcript to wangfy@nankai.edu.cn.
